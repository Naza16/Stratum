How the Appeals Process Works

1

Submit Appeal

File your appeal within 30 days of the negative action with supporting evidence and detailed reasoning. Remember that you can appeal a negative decision only once.

2

Initial Review

Our review team conducts an initial assessment of your appeal and supporting documentation within 5-7 business days.

3

Investigation

If eligible, a thorough investigation is conducted, including verification of evidence and circumstances. The process is strictly impartial and conducted by qualified assessors.

4

Decision

Final decision is made and communicated to you within 14-21 business days with detailed reasoning.
